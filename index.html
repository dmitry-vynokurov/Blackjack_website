Blackjack

Let's play

Object of the games is to score a number bigger than your oponent but not higher than 21.

The blackjack card values are:

  • Ace = 1 or 11
  • 2-9 = Face Value
  • 10, J, Q, K = 10

When both players have equal score, it is a draw. If one player has a score higher than 21 he loses.

Press "Hit" to draw a card. Draw cards until decired score or over 21. Press "Stand" to let your opponent play. Press "Deal to record a score and clear the table.

To hide rules start a game by pressing "Hit" or press "Rules". You can always revisit rules by pressing "Rules"
